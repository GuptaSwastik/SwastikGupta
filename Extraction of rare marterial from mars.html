Extraction rare material from mars

Extracting rare materials from Mars is being explored through various methods, including biomining, In-Situ Resource Utilization (ISRU), and sample return missions. Biomining uses microorganisms to leach valuable metals from Martian regolith, potentially for in-situ use. ISRU aims to utilize Martian resources for construction and other purposes, with iron extraction from regolith being a key focus. Sample return missions, like the one led by NASA and ESA, aim to bring samples back to Earth for detailed analysis and scientific discovery
